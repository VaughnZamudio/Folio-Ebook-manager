What is this?

Folio uses Supabase (free tier) as its backend. Create a free project, grab your URL and anon key from Project Settings → API, then paste them above.

In Supabase go to Authentication → Providers → Email and make sure "Confirm email" is disabled — that way you can sign up and log in instantly with no email step. Your data is locked to your account only via Row Level Security.

💡 How it works
  • Choose a book from your library using the dropdown on the left.
  • Upload a new cover image (JPG or PNG, ideally 6×9 ratio — about 1600×2400 px).
  • Click Apply Cover. The new art is saved to your Supabase library.
  • Re-download the epub and re-sideload to your device.
📐 Cover tips

Kindle Paperwhite displays covers at 1236 × 1648 px. Use a high-res JPG at that ratio for the sharpest result. Good sources: Open Library, TheCoverDB, or Canva.

📋 Setup guide
  • Go to Amazon → Manage Content → Preferences → Personal Document Settings.
  • Find your Kindle's Send-to-Kindle email address.
  • Add your sender email to the Approved list.
  • Enter both in ⚙ Kindle Settings, then select books and send.
