💡 How it works
  • Choose a book from your library using the dropdown on the left.
  • Upload a new cover image (JPG or PNG, ideally 6×9 ratio — about 1600×2400 px).
  • Click Apply Cover. Folio will embed the new image into the epub's metadata.
  • Re-download the updated epub from the library and re-sideload to any device.
📐 Cover tips

Kindle Paperwhite displays covers at 1236 × 1648 px (roughly 3:4). For the sharpest result, use a high-res JPG at that ratio. PNG works too but makes slightly larger files.

Good sources for cover art: Open Library, TheCoverDB, or custom art via Canva.

📋 Setup guide
  • Go to Amazon → Manage Your Content and Devices → Preferences → Personal Document Settings.
  • Find your Kindle's Send-to-Kindle Email address (ends in @kindle.com).
  • Under Approved Personal Document E-mail List, add the email you'll send from.
  • Enter both addresses in ⚙ Kindle Settings above, then select books and hit Send.
