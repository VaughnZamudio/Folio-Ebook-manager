📥 How to send
  • Select the books you want (click to toggle).
  • Click Download Selected — each epub saves to your computer. Custom covers are embedded automatically.
  • Click Open Send to Kindle and sign in with Amazon.
  • Drag & drop the downloaded files onto the page and click Send.
  • Done — books appear in your Kindle library in seconds.
📱 Open Send to Kindle ↗
💡 Tips

Amazon supports EPUB, PDF, DOCX, TXT and more, up to 200 MB per file. Books with a custom cover will have it embedded in the downloaded file.
